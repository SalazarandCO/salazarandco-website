✦ How we build

We don't build alone — and we don't build the same project twice.

Every UBIICA project is structured around the partners who fit that specific deal. Capital that understands the play. Brand and operating partners who elevate the asset. Lenders, architects, GCs and counsel chosen because they sharpen the outcome, not because they were last on the rolodex.

It's how we've taken a Coral Gables office assemblage to disposition, brought Marriott Bonvoy into a first-of-its-kind Orlando product, and put institutional capital behind ground-up residential across South Florida.

We value the few. We build with the right.
